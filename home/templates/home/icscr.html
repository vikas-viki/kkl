INSTITUTIONAL COMMITTEE FOR STEM CELL RESEARCH (ICSCR)

Scope and Functions

To review and approve research application involving human stem cell research according to the national guidelines published by ICMR. All research proposals/studies involving human stem cells conducted at or in collaboration with KaviKrishna Lab since 2012 should be reviewed and approved by IC-SCR prior to commencement of the project.
