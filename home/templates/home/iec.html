INSTITUTIONAL ETHICS COMMITTEE (IEC)

Scope and Functions

To review and approve research applications involving human laboratory and clinical research according to the national guidelines published by ICMR. All research proposals/studies involving human cell lines or clinical samples conducted at or in collaboration with KaviKrishna Lab since 2012 should be reviewed by IEC to confirm that such research was conducted as per national and international guidelines for laboratory and clinical research.
